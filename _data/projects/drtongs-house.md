  Nestled on a spacious 5-rai plot in Roi Et, the Doctor's House project is conveniently located near the iconic Roiet tower "หอโหวด" landmark. This home features 5 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms, designed to accommodate families of all ages.

  The concept blends gallery aesthetics, warmth, and garden elements, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Each room offers a unique view of the expansive garden surrounding the house. Elevated to catch the breeze, the central hall enjoys a refreshing flow of cool air throughout the day.

  This design not only emphasizes simplicity and elegance but also ensures a harmonious connection with nature, making it a perfect retreat for those who appreciate both comfort and style.
